Relational Plots
################

ScatterPlots:
	Scatter plots are good to see correlations within data
	
Line Plots:
	represents the change of one variable over a continuoust variable
	

Distribution Plots
##################

Histogram:
	Counts the observations and places them in discrete bins
	
KDE (Kernel Density Estimate) Plot:
	Visualisizes the distribution of observations in a dataset. 
	KDE represents the data using a continuous probability density curve
